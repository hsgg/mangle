balkanize is making unexpectedly vast numbers of polygons

Diagnosis: balkanize thinks that the input polygons are much larger than they really are, so there are many more intersections than there should be.

Solution: Double-check the units, and the format in general. For example, the h unit tells balkanize that the RA and Dec in the input file(s) are in hms and ° ′ ″ respectively, and that any other angle, such as the radius of a circular field, is in degrees.
