Mangle won't compile

This is bound to happen in the early days of a release, before mangle has been tested over a wide range of systems. Please try downloading a package with the precompiled binaries - you might not need to compile mangle yourself. If you are having trouble, please contact us, and we'll do what we can to help.

Mangle requires both C and fortran 77 compilers. The real*10 version requires a fortran compiler that supports real*10, such as gfortran or g95. Note that not all distributions of gfortran include real*10 support - if you get errors due to this, try the real*8 version or download the precompiled real*10 binaries.

Mangle is known to compile with the gnu suite of compilers, namely with gcc, g77 (gfortran for real*10), and gmake.
