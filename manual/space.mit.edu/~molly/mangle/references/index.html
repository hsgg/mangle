The scheme that evolved into mangle began life in the delightful atmosphere of an Aspen Center for Physics workshop in 1985. The mathematics of harmonization and other aspects of the computation of angular integrals are written up in the Appendix of Hamilton (1993) ApJ 417, 19-35. The mathematics of balkanization and other features of mangle are described by Hamilton & Tegmark (2004), MNRAS 349, 115-128. In 2007, Molly Swanson, Max Tegmark, and Colin Hill updated mangle to quickly handle the latest generation of galaxy survey masks. The updates, including pixelization and the interface with HEALPix, are described in Swanson et al. (2008), MNRAS 387, 1391-1402.
